SIEMENS MOBILITY

ACE Plan

Designed By: MSDAC

1. Axle counter evaluators process signals from axle counters to determine train occupancy in track sections.
2. Track sections are divided into blocks delimited by axle counters.
3. Evaluator units in control centers analyze signals and detect anomalies.
4. Occupancy status is determined for each block, crucial for train control.
5. Maintenance and monitoring ensure proper functioning of axle counter evaluators.
