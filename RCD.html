SIEMENS MOBILITY

Railway Consumer Depots

Designed By: MSDAC

1. Railway Consumer Depots (RCDs) store and distribute High-Speed Diesel Oil (HSD) for diesel locomotives in Indian Railways.
2. They maintain fuel quality, infrastructure, and comply with safety and environmental regulations.
3. RCDs coordinate with operational schedules and ensure timely refueling to prevent disruptions.
4. Inspections and audits are conducted to ensure safe and efficient functioning.
5. RCDs play a vital role in reliable and uninterrupted operation of diesel locomotives in the railway network.
