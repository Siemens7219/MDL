SIEMENS MOBILITY

Station Working Rule (SWR)

Designed By: Railway

1. SWR ensures safe operations at stations, covering line clear, signal reception, dispatch, level crossings, shunting, and communication during failures.
2. SWR must be concise and unambiguous, separate from general rules.
3. DSO, DSTE, and DEE can modify it; review every 5 years or with 5 amendment slips.
4. Staff in independent duties must sign a declaration register showing their understanding.
5. SWR enhances safety and regulates station operations.
