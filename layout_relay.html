S&T Equipment Layout Of Relay Room

Designed By: EI-SRAPL

1. Relay rooms in trains house signaling and telecommunication equipment for safe train movements and track control.
2. Signaling equipment includes interlocking systems, track circuits, signals, switches, and points, ensuring efficient train operations.
3. Telecommunication equipment facilitates communication between trains, control centers, and dispatchers.
4. Radios and network equipment handle communication and data transmission respectively.
5. CCTV systems in relay rooms monitor critical areas for safety and security purposes.
